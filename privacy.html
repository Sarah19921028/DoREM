Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. DoREM does not collect or store any of your personal data or dream entries on our servers. All information, including your past entries, is stored locally in your browser's memory using `localStorage`.

This means your data remains on your device and is not accessible to anyone else, including the developers of this website. You can clear this data at any time by clearing your browser's cache and local storage.

By using this website, you consent to the storage of your data on your local device.
