Welcome to the Diary of REM

The purpose of DoREM is to track specific elements and themes that reoccur in dreams. This website does not save any information therefore anything added to this site will only be saved to your device. Only you can see what you have saved. Use this site often if you wish to bridge your conscionce with the unconcscios. The more you record your dreams the more your dreams will reveal. For now, this is what has been built and this project is an may forever be a work in progress. If you have questions, I would love to answer them! Please reach out on INSTAGRAM.

To use this site is very simple...

  • Click on "Add Dream" to record a new dream.
  • Fill in the details of your dream, including themes and elements.
  • Save your dream to your device for future reference.
  • Use the Dream Dictionary to find meanings of common dream symbols.

    The Dream Dictionary

    The Dream Dictionary is a resource to help you interpret the symbols and themes in your dreams. It contains a list of common dream elements along with their possible meanings. Use this dictionary to gain insights into your subconscious mind and better understand the messages your dreams may be conveying.

    Keep in mind the definitions provided are common interpretations and there are symbols in your own dreams that may mean something personal to you. For example... dreaming about flying may be fun for some and terrifying for others. It is encouraged to use the Dream Dictionary highlighting tool to track repeating symbols or reoccuring dreams to help track and understand the personal connection to specific elements.
